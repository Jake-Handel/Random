About Us

'Les Petit Joyeux' is ultimately, a very simple premise. Translating to "Little Happiness", this website compiles a selection of toys from the Powerhouse Museum. Our aim is give people an insight into the evolution of toys over the years, and bring a little touch of happiness and nostalgia into the everyday.

All images and information on this website was accessed through the Powerhouse Mseum, accessible at the link included below. The PowerHouse Museum is located in Sydney, Australia and is home to over half a million objects deemed to be of signficance to Australians.
